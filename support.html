How to Play

Queens Tile Game is a logic puzzle where you place queens on a grid so that:

  • No two queens share the same row
  • No two queens share the same column

Frequently Asked Questions

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

  • Make sure you are running the latest version.
  • Close the app completely and reopen it.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app (this may reset progress).

Can I restore my progress?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or switch devices, progress cannot currently be transferred.

How do I remove ads?

Ads help support the continued development of the game. There is currently no ad-free option available.

Is the app available on Android?

Queens Tile Game is currently available on iOS. Other platforms may be considered in the future.
