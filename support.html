How to Play

Queens Tile Game is a logic puzzle where you place queens on a grid so that no two queens share the same row, column, or colored region. Each colored region must contain exactly one queen, and queens cannot touch each other — not even diagonally.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

  • Make sure you are running the latest version of the app.
  • Try closing the app completely and reopening it.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app. Note that this may reset your game progress.

Can I restore my progress?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or switch devices, your progress cannot be transferred at this time.

How do I remove ads?

Currently, ads help support the development of the game. There is no ad-free option available at this time.

Is the app available on Android?

Queens Tile Game is currently available on iOS. We may consider other platforms in the future.
