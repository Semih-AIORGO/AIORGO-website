Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Queens Tile Game ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by AIORGO. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The app does not require account creation or login.

The following non-personal data may be collected automatically:

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the app. This includes events such as levels started, levels completed, and app usage patterns. A randomly generated anonymous identifier is stored locally on your device to help us analyze usage trends. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Device Information: General device information such as device model, operating system version, and language settings may be collected by our third-party services for analytics and ad delivery purposes.

How We Use Information

  • To understand app usage and improve the game experience
  • To display advertisements
  • To diagnose technical issues

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob

Data Storage

Game progress and preferences (such as current level and theme selection) are stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
