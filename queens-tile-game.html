Queens Tile Game

A clean and strategic logic puzzle experience.

Place queens on the board so that no two queens share the same row, column, or colored region. Each region must contain exactly one queen, and queens cannot touch — not even diagonally.

Designed for players who enjoy thoughtful gameplay, structured challenges, and elegant visual design.
