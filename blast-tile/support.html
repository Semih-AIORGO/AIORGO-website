Thank you for playing Blast Tile!! If you need help or have any questions, you're in the right place.

How to Play

Blast Tile! is a fast-paced match puzzle game. Tap groups of matching colored tiles to blast them off the board. Chain combos for higher scores. Choose between Endless mode for freeform play or Mission mode to complete specific score and move goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

  • Make sure you are running the latest version of the app.
  • Try closing the app completely and reopening it.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app. Note that this may reset your settings.

Can I restore my progress?

Settings and preferences (theme, language, sound) are stored securely on your device. If you uninstall the app, these settings will be reset.

How do rewarded ads work?

In certain situations you may be offered the option to watch a short ad in exchange for an in-game benefit. Watching the ad is always optional.

How do I change the tile theme or language?

Tap the settings icon (gear) in the top right corner of the home screen. From there you can switch tile themes, language, sound, and vibration settings.

Is the app available on Android?

Blast Tile! is currently available on iOS. We may consider Android in the future.
